BANK HIRING MANUAL FOR INTERNS

COMPANY: BALFIN Group.
LAST UPDATED: February 2026

========================================
INTERN POSITION REQUIREMENTS
========================================

EDUCATION:
- Must have Bachelor's degree (in progress or completed)
- Preferred fields: Finance, Economics, Business Administration, Accounting
- Minimum GPA: 3.0 out of 4.0
- Graduate students are welcome to apply

AGE & ELIGIBILITY:
- Must be 18-25 years old
- Must be legally authorized to work
- Students in their final year or recent graduates preferred

EXPERIENCE:
- Minimum 1 previous internship or relevant work experience
- Financial services experience is a strong plus
- Volunteer or leadership experience counts

SKILLS (REQUIRED):
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Word
- Basic understanding of financial concepts

SKILLS (PREFERRED):
- Knowledge of Python, R, or SQL
- Experience with data analysis tools
- Familiarity with financial software (Bloomberg, etc.)
- Second language (especially Spanish or Mandarin)

========================================
EVALUATION CRITERIA
========================================

We evaluate candidates based on the following weights:

1. Education & Academic Performance: 35%
2. Relevant Experience: 30%
3. Technical Skills: 20%
4. Soft Skills & Communication: 15%

SCORING SYSTEM:
- 90-100: Highly Recommended (Fast-track interview)
- 75-89: Recommended (Standard interview)
- 60-74: Consider (Second review needed)
- Below 60: Not Recommended

========================================
AUTOMATIC DISQUALIFIERS
========================================

The following will result in immediate rejection:
- Criminal record involving fraud or financial crimes
- GPA below 2.5
- False information on resume or application
- No relevant education (non-business degree without explanation)
- Unable to work legally in the country

========================================
RED FLAGS (REVIEW CAREFULLY)
========================================

- Frequent job hopping (more than 3 jobs in 2 years)
- Unexplained gaps in education or employment
- No references provided
- Poor grammar or unprofessional resume format
- Lack of any extracurricular activities or leadership

========================================
POSITIVE INDICATORS
========================================

Look for these strong signals:
- Dean's List or academic honors
- Leadership roles in student organizations
- Relevant coursework (Corporate Finance, Investments, etc.)
- Certifications (CFA Level 1, Financial Modeling, etc.)
- Prior banking or finance internships
- Participation in case competitions or finance clubs
- Strong recommendations from professors or employers

========================================
INTERVIEW INVITATION CRITERIA
========================================

Automatically invite to interview if:
- GPA 3.5 or higher with finance degree
- Previous internship at top financial institution
- Strong technical skills (Python/SQL) + finance background
- Exceptional leadership experience

Require manager approval before inviting if:
- GPA between 2.8-3.0
- Non-traditional background (career change)
- International students requiring visa sponsorship
- Candidates with only 1 semester remaining

========================================
FINAL NOTES
========================================

Remember: We're looking for candidates who show:
1. Strong analytical thinking
2. Genuine interest in finance/banking
3. Cultural fit with our team values
4. Growth potential and learning mindset

When in doubt, prioritize candidates who demonstrate curiosity, 
work ethic, and alignment with our company values over pure 
technical skills, which can be taught.
